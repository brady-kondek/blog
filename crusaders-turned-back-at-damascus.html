Crusaders Turned Back at Damascus
  • July 28, 1148

BREAKING NEWS: CRUSADERS TURNED BACK AT DAMASCUS


This just in, we are getting reports that the Crusaders, being led by Louis VII and Conrad III, have been turned back at Damascus and have abandoned the city. From what we have heard, the crusaders launched their attack from the west, since there were orchards that provided a steady food supply for the time being. They then moved on to the east side of the city, but that ended up with the local crusader ords refusing to go forward with the siege. This resulted in the abandoning of the city. This may not look good on the Crusaders in the future if they ever assemble together again.
