Frederick I Dies
  • June 10, 1190

BREAKING NEWS: FREDERICK I, HOLY ROMAN EMPEROR, DIES ON THE WAY TO THE HOLY LAND


We are receiving reports that, on the way to the Holy Land, the Holy Roman Emperor Frederick I has died. The emperor had received advice to follow a shortcut along the Saleph river. He decided to take it, however drowned while taking the shortcut. From what we are hearing, most of his troops will be returning home from their duties.
