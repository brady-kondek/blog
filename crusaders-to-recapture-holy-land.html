Crusaders to Recapture Holy Land
  • November 27, 1095

BREAKING NEWS: POPE URBAN II ORDERS CRUSADERS TO RECAPTURE HOLY LAND


This just in, Pope Urban II has just called on all Christians to war against the Muslims to regain control of the Holy Land. For some context into this, let me explain. Christians have made pilgrimages to the Holy Land since the 6th century, but that didn't last when the Seljuk Turks took control. When they took control, Christians were prohibited. On top of that, the Seljuk Turks even threatened to invade Constantinople and take control of it. The Christians hope that this plan goes through successfully in order to protect Christian sites from Muslim control.
