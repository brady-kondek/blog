Jerusalem Recaptured by Crusaders
  • July 14, 1099

BREAKING NEWS: JERUSALEM CAPTURED BY CRUSADERS


After seven weeks of siege, European Christian knights have captured the city of Jerusalem from the Turks. It was a long journey through the various mountains along the way, but they made it. The crusaders reached the city on June 7th, but had a bit of a change in plans when they got there. When they reached the city, they found that it was very protected. So they built siege towers, and by July 13th they were ready to continue their plan. Just today though, the Gate of Saint Stephen was opened, which let the soldiers and knights in, and the city was captured.
