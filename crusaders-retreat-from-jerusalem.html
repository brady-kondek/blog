Crusaders Forced to Retreat From Jerusalem
  • June 1, 1192

BREAKING NEWS: CRUSADERS FORCED TO RETREAT FROM JERUSALEM


We are receiving reports that the crusaders have been forced to retreat from Jerusalem. The army had made an advance towards Jerusalem, and came in sight of the city. However, disputes among leaders resulting in the retreat. The army was split into two, however this made it impossible to attack Jerusalem as neither one alone was strong enough to reach the outcome they wanted.
